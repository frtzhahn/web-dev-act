Tempura

Light and crispy Japanese deep fried seafood and vegetables

Prep: 20 min Cook: 15 min Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 sweet potato, sliced thin
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 8 shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 bunch broccoli florets
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup ice cold water
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup dashi stock
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp grated daikon radish
  • 1 tsp grated ginger

Instructions

  1. Make dipping sauce by mixing dashi, soy sauce, and mirin
  2. Heat to boil then cool, add daikon and ginger
  3. Heat oil in deep pot to 350 degrees F
  4. Mix egg yolk with ice cold water
  5. Add flour and mix lightly with chopsticks, batter should be lumpy
  6. Pat vegetables and shrimp completely dry
  7. Dip shrimp in batter, let excess drip off
  8. Carefully place in hot oil, fry for 2-3 minutes
  9. Remove when light golden and drain on paper towels
  10. Repeat with vegetables, cooking similar sized items together
  11. Serve immediately while hot and crispy
  12. Dip in sauce before eating

Cooking Tips

Keep batter ice cold for crispy coating, add ice cubes if needed. Don't overmix batter, lumps are essential. Fry in small batches to maintain oil temperature. Score shrimp on belly side to prevent curling. Best served immediately after frying.
