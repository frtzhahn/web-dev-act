Adobo

The classic Filipino dish with savory soy and vinegar sauce

Prep: 15 min Cook: 45 min Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken or pork, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken or pork in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat
  3. Remove meat from marinade and brown on all sides
  4. Pour in the marinade and add bay leaves
  5. Add water and bring to a boil
  6. Reduce heat and simmer covered for 30-40 minutes until meat is tender
  7. Remove lid and simmer for another 10 minutes to thicken sauce
  8. Adjust seasoning with salt and sugar if needed
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice

Cooking Tips

For best flavor, marinate overnight in the refrigerator. You can use chicken, pork, or a combination of both. Add hard boiled eggs during the last 5 minutes of cooking for extra protein. The longer you simmer, the more tender and flavorful the meat becomes.
