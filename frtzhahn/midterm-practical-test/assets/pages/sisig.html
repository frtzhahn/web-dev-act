Sisig

Sizzling crispy pork with onions and spices

Prep: 30 min Cook: 45 min Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork face or pork belly
  • 1/2 lb chicken liver
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3-4 pieces green chili peppers, chopped
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Calamansi or lemon for serving

Instructions

  1. Boil pork in water with salt for 30 minutes until tender
  2. Grill or broil pork until skin is crispy
  3. Chop pork into small pieces
  4. Boil chicken liver for 5 minutes, then chop finely
  5. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat
  6. Saut√© onions until translucent
  7. Add chopped pork and liver, cook for 5 minutes
  8. Add soy sauce and vinegar, mix well
  9. Add chili peppers and mayonnaise, stir to combine
  10. Transfer to a sizzling plate, top with raw egg
  11. Serve immediately with calamansi and rice

Cooking Tips

For crispy sisig, make sure to grill pork until very crispy. Traditionally served on a hot sizzling plate which cooks the egg. Add more chili for extra spice. Best enjoyed with beer. The raw egg on top gets cooked by the hot sizzling plate.
