Okonomiyaki

Japanese savory pancake with cabbage and toppings

Prep: 15 min Cook: 20 min Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups dashi stock or water
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 cups cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 lb pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tempura scraps (tenkasu)
  • 1/4 cup pickled red ginger
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • Japanese mayonnaise
  • Bonito flakes
  • Aonori seaweed powder
  • Vegetable oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and dashi in large bowl until smooth
  2. Add eggs and whisk until combined
  3. Fold in cabbage, green onions, and tempura scraps
  4. Heat oil in large pan over medium heat
  5. Pour one quarter of batter into pan, shape into circle
  6. Place pork belly slices on top
  7. Cook for 5 minutes until bottom is golden
  8. Flip carefully and cook other side for 5 minutes
  9. Transfer to plate, pork side up
  10. Drizzle with okonomiyaki sauce and mayonnaise
  11. Sprinkle with bonito flakes and aonori
  12. Top with pickled ginger and serve hot

Cooking Tips

Don't overmix the batter, lumps are okay. Press down gently while cooking for even thickness. You can add shrimp, squid, or cheese for variation. Make sauce with worcestershire sauce, ketchup, and sugar if okonomiyaki sauce unavailable.
