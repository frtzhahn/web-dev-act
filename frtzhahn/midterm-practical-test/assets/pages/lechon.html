Lechon

Roasted whole pig with crispy golden skin

Prep: 2 hours Cook: 4-5 hours Servings: 15-20

Ingredients

  • 1 whole pig (20-25 lbs) or pork belly (5 lbs)
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 2 heads garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 2 tbsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 6 stalks lemongrass
  • 10 bay leaves
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, salt, pepper, and sugar to make marinade
  2. Rub marinade all over the pork inside and out
  3. Stuff cavity with lemongrass, bay leaves, and green onions
  4. Let marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F or prepare charcoal grill
  6. Place pork on a roasting rack
  7. Roast for 4-5 hours, basting with oil every 30 minutes
  8. Increase heat to 400 degrees F for last 30 minutes for crispy skin
  9. Let rest for 15 minutes before carving
  10. Serve with lechon sauce or liver sauce

Cooking Tips

For crispy skin, make sure pork is completely dry before roasting. Score the skin lightly to help render fat. If using a whole pig, traditional method uses charcoal roasting on a spit for 4-5 hours. Basting regularly ensures moist meat and crispy skin.
