Sushi

Traditional Japanese vinegared rice with fresh fish

Prep: 45 min Cook: 30 min Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 3 cups sushi rice
  • 3 1/2 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 8 oz sashimi grade salmon
  • 8 oz sashimi grade tuna
  • 4 oz imitation crab sticks
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed
  • Soy sauce for serving
  • Wasabi paste
  • Pickled ginger

Instructions

  1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear
  2. Cook rice with water in rice cooker or pot
  3. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved
  4. Transfer cooked rice to large bowl
  5. Pour vinegar mixture over rice and fold gently
  6. Fan rice while mixing to cool it down
  7. Cover with damp towel until ready to use
  8. Slice fish into thin strips
  9. Place nori on bamboo mat, shiny side down
  10. Spread rice evenly on nori, leave 1 inch at top
  11. Add fish, cucumber, and avocado in a line
  12. Roll tightly using bamboo mat
  13. Cut into 8 pieces with sharp wet knife
  14. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger

Cooking Tips

Use only sashimi grade fish for raw sushi. Keep hands wet to prevent rice from sticking. Sharp knife is essential for clean cuts. Make inside out rolls by flipping nori after spreading rice. Store unused rice covered with damp towel.
