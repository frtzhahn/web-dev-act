Sinigang

Tangy and savory Filipino tamarind soup

Prep: 15 min Cook: 30 min Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork belly or shrimp
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 pack sinigang mix or 10-12 pieces tamarind
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 1 medium radish, sliced
  • 2 long green peppers
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 10 pieces string beans, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 6 cups water
  • Fish sauce to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil
  2. Add pork, onion, and tomatoes
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes until pork is tender
  4. Add radish and eggplant, cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add sinigang mix and stir well
  6. Add string beans and peppers, cook for 3 minutes
  7. Add kangkong and cook for 1 minute
  8. Season with fish sauce to taste
  9. Turn off heat and let stand for 2 minutes
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice

Cooking Tips

Don't overcook vegetables to keep them crunchy. You can substitute pork with fish, shrimp, or beef. For authentic sour taste, use fresh tamarind instead of sinigang mix. Add vegetables in order of cooking time, hardest vegetables first.
