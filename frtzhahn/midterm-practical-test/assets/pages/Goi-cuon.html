Gỏi cuốn

Fresh Vietnamese spring rolls with shrimp and herbs

Prep: 30 min Cook: 10 min Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 12 rice paper wrappers
  • 12 large shrimp, cooked and halved
  • 4 oz rice vermicelli noodles
  • 1 cup lettuce leaves
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup Thai basil leaves
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package directions, drain and set aside
  2. Boil shrimp for 3-4 minutes until pink, cool and cut in half lengthwise
  3. Prepare all vegetables and herbs, arrange on platter
  4. Fill large bowl with warm water
  5. Dip one rice paper in water for 3 seconds until soft
  6. Place on clean surface, let rest for 30 seconds
  7. Layer lettuce, noodles, shrimp, herbs, cucumber, and carrots in center
  8. Fold bottom edge over filling, fold in sides
  9. Roll tightly away from you to seal
  10. Make peanut sauce by mixing hoisin, peanut butter, soy sauce, and garlic
  11. Add water to thin if needed
  12. Serve rolls immediately with peanut sauce and crushed peanuts

Cooking Tips

Don't oversoak rice paper or it will tear. Work with one wrapper at a time. Place shrimp on top so it shows through wrapper. Keep finished rolls covered with damp towel. Best eaten fresh, don't refrigerate as rice paper hardens.
