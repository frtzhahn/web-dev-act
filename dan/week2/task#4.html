4 Components of the Computer System

Hardware
-Computer hardware refers to the physical components that make up a computer system.
Software
-Software is a collection of ictions, data, or computer programs that enable a computer to perform specific tasks.
Dataware
-A central repository for storing and analyzing structured and semi-structured data from multiple sources, used for business intelligence and reporting.
Peopleware/Liveware
-Peopleware/Liveware refers to the human element of computer systems, including the people, skills, and processes involved in developing, using, and managing hardware and software.
